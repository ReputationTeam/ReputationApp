Our Journey

How we turned an idea into an app.

  • 9 a.m. to 11 a.m.

    How We Began

    We brainstormed ideas for around sixty minutes and then decided to make an app called Reputation to track followers and following across social platforms!

  • 11 a.m. to 1 p.m.

    Reputation is Born

    During this time period, we learned how to obtain API's from different social platforms and realized how much of a challenge this would be, but we were up for it!

  • 1 p.m. to 5 p.m.

    Work in Progress

    For four hours, we worked away at our code, each of us responsible for a different aspect for the app!

  • 5 p.m. to 9 p.m.

    Success

    We accomplished what we had originally planned, overcoming many obstacles, learning in the process, and are proud to have come so far!
